# Xiaohongshu Feed Strategy

Every Xiaohongshu cover starts with one question:

```text
用户为什么停下来？
```

## Five First-Image Patterns

### 1. Real Proof

Use real photos, screenshots, result images, or product states as the visual proof.

Best for reviews, tools, recipes, travel, home, results.

### 2. Big Title Stop

Use a short high-contrast Chinese title that remains readable at 25% size.

Best for tutorials, tips, workplace, tools, relationship observations.

### 3. Result First

Show the result before explaining the process.

Signals: `省时间`, `避坑`, `直接出`, `终于`, `不用再`.

### 4. Emotion First

Use a human-sounding emotion hook, not a marketing slogan.

Good: `每天纠结穿什么的人有救了`

Bad: `震惊全网的神器来了`

### 5. Screenshot Proof

For AI tools, apps, workflows, and tutorials, enlarge real UI/screenshots. Do not use a robot illustration as proof.

## Strength Level

Most Xiaohongshu covers should be level 3-4:

1. quiet
2. polished explanation
3. strong social card
4. feed-native hook
5. high-impact bomb

Level 5 is rare. Do not default to noisy.
