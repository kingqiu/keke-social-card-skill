# Xiaohongshu Cover Quality

Use this together with `aesthetic-standards.md`. Passing thumbnail readability is necessary but not sufficient; the whole card set must also feel publishable.

## Blocking Issues

- Title unreadable at thumbnail size.
- No clear reason to click.
- Proof/result image too small.
- Screenshot treated as decoration.
- Pretty but vague.
- Title promises something the card does not show.

## Thumbnail Test

At 25% size, the viewer should still know:

1. What this is.
2. Why it is worth tapping.
3. Where the evidence/result is.

## Title Rules

- Prefer 6-18 Chinese characters for the cover hook.
- Avoid more than 3 title lines.
- Use a safe text zone over busy photos.
- Do not cover faces, product logo, food subject, or key UI.

## Image Rules

- Evidence/result image must be big enough to inspect.
- Set `object-position` intentionally.
- Use localized backing/tint only when needed.
- Do not use abstract AI art where proof is needed.
